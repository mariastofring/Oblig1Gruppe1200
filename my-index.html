Pasta

Pasta alla carbonara

Pasta alla carbonara is a famous Italian pasta dish. With grated parmesan, eggs and cream you get the best pasta sauce of them all. With the pasta dish you will get a slice of foccacia with aioli and pesto of your choosing.

Allergens: Eggs, gluten, milk, soy and celery.

Penne al pollo ubriaco

Penne al pollo ubriaco is a classic pasta that is suitable for both children and adults. The pasta dish consists of penne, chicken, bacon, tomato, garlic, basil, peperoncini, cream and parsley. You will also get a slice of foccacia with aioli and pesto of your choosing.

Allergens: Gluten, milk, celery and soy.

Pizza

Pizza Margarita

Invented in the 1800s, pizza margherita showcases the colors of the Italian flag: red from the tomato sauce, white from the mozzarella, and green from the basil.

Allergens: Gluten, milk.

Pizza Capricciosa

Pizza capricciosa (Italian pronunciation: [ˈpittsa kapritˈtʃoːza]) is a style of pizza in Italian cuisine prepared with mozzarella cheese, Italian baked ham, mushroom, artichoke and tomato.

Allergens: Gluten, milk and soy.

Pizza Parma

The pizza lovers' favorite is made from few, but extra good ingredients! Crispy pizza bottom with crème fraîche, parma ham, juicy cherry tomatoes, artichoke and arugula salad.

Allergens: Gluten, sulfit and milk.

Dessert

Tiramisu

An italian classic, tiramisu. This fantastic classic is made with savoiard biscuits dipped in coffee and macarpone cream.

Allergens: Gluten, eggs, suflit and milk.

Gelato o Sorbeto

Two scoops of the best homemade Italian gelato or sorbet you'll ever taste. We offer two kinds of gelato, chocolate and vanilla. In the sorbet section we offer the fantastic mango- and passion sorbet and a delicious lemon sorbet.

Allergens: Milk.
